    About Us

    Welcome to HealthBot, an AI-driven public health chatbot designed to raise awareness about diseases and promote healthier lifestyles. Using advanced artificial intelligence, our chatbot provides reliable information, guidance on symptoms, preventive measures, and general health tips. Our goal is to empower individuals with accurate health knowledge and encourage timely medical consultation, making healthcare information accessible to everyone, anytime, anywhere.

    HealthBot is ideal for educational purposes, community health initiatives, and supporting individuals in understanding and managing their well-being effectively.
